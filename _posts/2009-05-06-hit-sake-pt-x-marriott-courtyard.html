The meeting was great, some really, really interesting people, and a nice crisp pace to discussions, agreements and actions. I hope I picked up a few hints! The hotel is in a suburb of Paris, but close enough to walk the L'arc de Triomphe, Champs Elysees, etc. The other great thing about the trip was just how good the Eurostar is now, especially that the St. Pancras terminal is fully functional. The only thing to watch out for are all the 'helpers' at Gare de Nord when you arrive - I didn't, but I will next time! Oh, and now the pound and Euro have parity, it is so, so expensive on the continent.
