--- layout: post title: ChEMBL Internships date: '2009-09-27T13:42:00.003+01:00' author: John Overington tags: - Staff and Recruitment modified_time: '2009-09-27T13:51:23.876+01:00' thumbnail: http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_SlPG9DIz_AA/Sr9fS_-TA2I/AAAAAAAAAhQ/srUxSR-uqiQ/s72-c/Durruti.png blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-2546008714740235720.post-3480551050987687912 blogger_orig_url: http://chembl.blogspot.com/2009/09/chembl-internships.html --- We regularly get request for internships in our group, and we are keen to host suitable scientists from a broad range of molecular informatics and IT backgrounds. These visits are typically for from two to three months in our lab here at the EMBL-EBI, and there is always lots of work to be done. We can provide modest financial support, but cannot cover travel costs to/from the UK.

If you wish to apply, please use this link and 1) attach a current and accurate cv/resume, 2) state when you are available to work, and 3) (MOST IMPORTANT) what you would like to achieve during your time with us.
