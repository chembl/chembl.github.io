This one day session was organised by the Canadian Bioinformatics Workshop. It was held at the Ontario Institute for Cancer Research, which is situated in downtown Toronto. It was a very well organised workshop with good facilities and tasty refreshments. Toronto is a beautiful city and I hope to explore it tomorrow before flying down to Boston to give my talk at Bio-IT World.
