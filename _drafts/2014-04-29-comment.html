Wikipedia: Dapagliflozin
ATC code: A10BX09

On January 8th 2014, FDA approved Dapagliflozin (tradename: Farxiga; research code: BMS-512148; ChEMBL: CHEMBL429910), a sodium-glucose cotransporter 2 (SGLT2) inhibitor indicated to improve glycemic control, along with diet and exercise, in adults with type 2 diabetes mellitus. Dapagliflozin is the second SGLT2 inhibitor to reach the US market, after the first-in-class Canagliflozin. However, Dapagliflozin was the first SGLT2 inhibitor approved anywhere in the world in 2011 by EU.
